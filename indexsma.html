Safety Mama

Tell Me More

Vision

SafetyMama is a system that helps you to attain a peaceful pregnancy in total safety. It collects and monitors data and information pertaining to your and your baby physical well-being. It is very important to walk, so a fitness tracker will monitor this aspect and give you automatic suggestions. It allows you to listen to the heartbeat and movements of the fetus in order to be able to check in every moment of the day well-being of your child. Moreover is important to control your anxiety and stress status, as they could damage the health of the fetus, so SMa recognize if you are under stress, through the values of your heart rate, your blood pressure and your body temperature, and if you are at home it shall make you relax automatically activating a music system, chromotherapy and aromatherapy, to try to raise your level of well-being. The weekly data collected will be automatically sent to your gynecologist, who will then be able to provide specific advice.

AMI STEPS

Acting

Your home tries to keep you relaxed with light, music, etc. activations in order to improve your "emotive" well-being)

Reasoning

Stress inference and activation of chromotherapy and aromatherapy system if the woman is at home.

Interacting

Suggestions and data browsing. The woman can turn of music, or lights, or frangances.

Sensing

Physical movement (walk), stress detection (heart rate, pressure, temperature, etc.), heartbeat and movement of the fetus.

AMI FEATURES

Intelligent

During chromotherapy and aromatherapy it verifies in real time the evolution of the parameters, and if there is no improvements proposes further investigations. The system notifies you if you have not walked enough.

Ubiquitous

----------

Sensitive

Able to sense woman's presence in the house, to measure heart rate, blood pressure, body temperature and able to listen to the heartbeat and movements of the fetus.>

Adaptive

The system sends data more frequently to the gynecologist, as you get closer to childbirth.

Responsive

Activation of music, lights and fragrances. Creation of graphs on the basis of the heartbeat.

Transparent

The system collects data and the woman does not notice (ex heart rate, number of steps). You must measure "manually" the heartbeat and the movements of the fetus, but it is an enjoyable and rewarding practice.
